ABOUT VIRAT

Virat Kohli (born November 5, 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and global sporting icon. Widely regarded as one of the greatest limited-overs batters in history, he is celebrated for his aggressive batting style, world-record 50 ODI centuries, and for revolutionizing fitness standards in Indian cricket. Virat Kohli is known for determination, passion, discipline and match-winning batting. He became one of the most recognised cricketers in modern cricket.
